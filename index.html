Welcome to HealthyEats!

This is the beginning of your journey to navigating nutritional health, satisfaction, and freedom.

This website is the result of a research project focused on creating a resource for immigrants in Canada that provides them with healthier food options and restaurant suggestions!

The cultural food inaccessibility in Canada is prevalent and studies show that a high percentage of immigrants rely on fast food and end up with diseases such as: heart disease, type 2 diabetes, and obesity. This website's aim is to provide not only healthier but similar meal options and suggestions that make you feel like you are back home!

Currently, only African food alternatives are available but the end goal is to expand and make it accessible to immigrants from all over the globe.
